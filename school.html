SUPPORTING TAILWIND CSS EDUCATION FOR EVERY CHILD IN NEW YORK CITY

Tailwindcss School NYC’s mission is to support tailwindcss education for every child in New York City. We partner with New York City public schools to cultivate healthy students and communities through hands-on cooking and gardening education, transforming children’s relationship with food.
